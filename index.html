The transplant program is very active. Longevo is your one-stop destination to book the best Nephrologists in your town.

Robotic-Surgery

Robotic Surgery is a branch of medical science that uses a robotic arm controlled with a computer by a surgeon. Small tools attached to the robotic arm are then used to perform the surgery by copying the hand movements of the surgeon as he controls the robotic arm. With this method being more convenient for the surgeon due to a perfect view of the area under surgery,the hospitals under Longevo Robotic Surgery Department is equipped with a skilled team of the best Surgeons. Longevo is your one-stop destination to book the finest Robot-assisted Surgeons in your town.

Bariatric Surgery

Bariatric Surgery deals with weight reduction using various techniques to combat obesity. The process includes reducing the size of the stomach or removing a portion of the stomach or re-routing the small intestine. Urology

Urology deals with diagnosis and treatment of diseases predominantly of male reproductive organs and the urinary tract of males and females. Hospitals under Longevo have state-of-the-art Urology facilities including minimally invasive surgeries, robotics and laparoscopy techniques, fiber-optic endoscopy, and lithotripsy units. An urologist deals with diseases such as Incontinence, prostate enlargement, kidney stones, prostate cancer, penile fractures, urinary infections, and STDs, just to name a few. The department works closely with uro-oncology, uro gynecology, nephrology, colorectal surgery, andrology to offer a more systematic and collaborative approach to treatment.
